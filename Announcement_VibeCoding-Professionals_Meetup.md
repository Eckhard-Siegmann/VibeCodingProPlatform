## Event Overview
This is an interactive meetup for developers featuring a 30-minute hackathon, BYOP (Bring Your Own Problem) sessions, and exchange with AI coding professionals. The focus is on interactive learning and exchange rather than long talks.

**Core Themes:**
* Code quality and algorithmic thinking
* Production-grade solutions
* Building complex systems with VibeCoding and requirements engineering
* Orchestrating departments of agents for quality and speed

## Who is this for?
Computer scientists, data scientists, engineers, and technical professionals who care deeply about code quality and mastering complex systems.
